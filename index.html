📖 How to Play

Goal: Be the first to get rid of all your cards.

On your turn: Play a card that matches the top card by COLOR or NUMBER. If you can't play, draw a card from the deck.

Special Cards:
🚫 Skip — Next player loses their turn
🔄 Reverse — Changes direction of play
➕ Draw 2 — Next player draws 2 and loses turn
🌈 Wild — Play on anything, pick a new color
🌈➕ Wild Draw 4 — Pick a color, next player draws 4

UNO! When you have 1 card left, press the UNO button! If someone catches you forgetting, you draw 2 penalty cards.
